Frequently Asked Questions

How does sponsorship work?

You set your monthly contribution on GitHub Sponsors. Funds are collected monthly and used to create better content.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can manage or cancel your sponsorship at any time, no questions asked.

What if I want to sponsor differently?

Contact us at hello@magicalpawskidstv.com for custom sponsorship options or partnerships.

How is the money used?

Sponsorship funds go towards: video production, animation, music licensing, equipment, and creating new magical shows.

Is my information safe?

Yes! GitHub Sponsors uses secure payment processing. We never sell your data.

Can businesses sponsor us?

Absolutely! We welcome corporate partnerships. Email hello@magicalpawskidstv.com with details.
