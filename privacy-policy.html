Introduction

Magical Paws Kids TV ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://magicalpawskidstv.com and our YouTube channel.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and other contact information you voluntarily provide
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address (for site analytics)
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and clickthrough data
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to enhance your experience

2. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website and YouTube content
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services
  • Send you updates about new content and episodes
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • Process sponsorships and payments through GitHub Sponsors

3. Children's Privacy

Protecting children's privacy is especially important to us. Our website and YouTube content are designed for families with children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent, in accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services, including:

  • Google Analytics: To track website usage and traffic
  • Google AdSense: To display family-friendly advertisements
  • GitHub Sponsors: To process sponsorship payments securely
  • YouTube: For video hosting and distribution

These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. You can control cookie settings through your browser. However, disabling cookies may affect certain website functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@magicalpawskidstv.com

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@magicalpawskidstv.com
  • Website: https://magicalpawskidstv.com

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

10. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold. You also have the right to delete personal information collected under certain circumstances and to opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. Please contact us to exercise these rights.
